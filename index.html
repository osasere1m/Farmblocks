The FB Token: Enabling Tangible Ownership

The FB token serves as the gateway to the world of FarmBlocks, granting holders ownership of a specific plot of Swedish farmland. As the first-ever real-world asset token, FB transcends the traditional boundaries of cryptocurrency by bridging the gap between the digital and physical realms. This ownership confers rights to potential agricultural income, conservation efforts, and an unprecedented sense of stewardship over the land.

FarmBlock Token - Token Utility

Beyond Economics - Crafting Tomorrow's Prosperity with a Sustaining Value Model

Land Ownership
FB tokens represent tangible ownership of a specific plot of Swedish farmland, verified on the blockchain. Ownership includes potential agricultural returns and participation in conservation efforts.
Voting Rights
FB token holders are empowered with voting rights, influencing land management practices and the project's strategic direction.
Staking and Rewards
Token holders can stake FB tokens, earning rewards such as a share of agricultural profits and ecological incentives.
Governance and Participation
Token holders can actively shape FarmBlocks' future by proposing and voting on initiatives through a decentralized governance model.

Bridging Blockchain and Real-World Assets

Beyond Economics - Crafting Tomorrow's Prosperity with a Sustaining Value Model

Agricultural Returns
A portion of profits generated from agricultural activities on Swedish farmland is distributed to token holders, ensuring a direct link between land's success and token value.
Buybacks and Burns
Periodic buybacks using project revenue maintain token scarcity, increasing value.
Ecosystem Development
Periodic buybacks using project revenue maintain token scarcity, increasing value. Some tokens are burned, enhancing scarcity and utility.
